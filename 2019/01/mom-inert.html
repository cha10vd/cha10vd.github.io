Matrix outer product:


The outer product is defined as thethe following vector product:
\begin{equation} \vec{u} \otimes \vec{v} = \vec{u}\vec{v^T} = \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \\ z \\ \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x & y & z \\ \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} x^{2} & xy & xz \\ yx & y^{2} & yz \\ zx & zy & z^{2} \\ \end{bmatrix} \end{equation}
